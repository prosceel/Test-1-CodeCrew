6 Great Ways To Save The Bees This Fall
While we can’t all be beekeepers, we can each do our bit to save the bees and keep the world sweet.
Help The Bees
Bee The Change
Bees are an essential part of our ecosystem, yet they’re facing a serious crisis worldwide. If you want to be part of the solution, here’s what you can do to help:
Donate: From as little as $10, you can help us educate future environmental stewards through our Hybrid Environmental STEM Program.
Start a Facebook Fundraiser: Get others buzzing about the magic of bees.
Support Bees While You Shop: Give back with every purchase at no extra cost to you, Amazon Smile, iGive, and Giving Assistant will donate a percentage of your purchase total.
Shop Planet Bee Merch: Shop Planet Bee’s stylish and comfy tees, hoodies, and tanks. All proceeds go towards our education programs.
Get fit for bees: You can nominate Planet Bee for a virtual 5k through Virtual Runs or Virtual Strides. Simply click on ‘nominate a charity’ on the Virtual Runs page or ‘contact us’ on Virtual Strides to get started.
Book a Planet Bee Corporate Team Building Program: Treat your team to one of Planet Bee’s engaging corporate team-building programs.
